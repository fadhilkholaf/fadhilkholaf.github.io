About Tsuda Shiori

Tsuda Shiori (津田詩織) is a central character in Shunji Iwai's acclaimed film "All About Lily Chou-Chou" (2001). As a classmate of the protagonist Yuichi, Shiori's experiences reflect the pain, alienation, and search for solace that define the film's haunting atmosphere. Her story is one of vulnerability and resilience amid the turbulence of adolescence.

Role in the Story

Shiori's journey in "All About Lily Chou-Chou" is marked by hardship and trauma. She becomes a victim of bullying and violence, yet her presence is a powerful reminder of the silent suffering many endure. Through her connection to Lily Chou-Chou's music, Shiori seeks escape and comfort, embodying the film's themes of longing and the search for hope in a harsh world.
